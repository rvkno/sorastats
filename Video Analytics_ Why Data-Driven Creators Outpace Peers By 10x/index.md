### The Five Core Metrics That Matter

**1. Completion Rate (Watch Time Quality)**





What percentage of your video do viewers actually watch? Average = 50%. Viral = 75%+.

Why it matters: YouTube prioritizes watch time. If viewers abandon halfway, the algorithm stops recommending your video.

**2. Click-Through Rate (CTR)**

**to Profile**

What percentage of viewers click your profile? Average = 0.8%. Good = 2-4%. Great = 6%+.

Why it matters: Profile visits = new subscriber opportunities. CTR signals content appeal.

**3. Share Rate**

What percentage share your video? Average = 0.3%. Viral = 2-4%.

Why it matters: Shares bypass the algorithm entirely. Direct shares = exponential reach.

**4. Save Rate (YouTube Playlists)**

What percentage add your video to playlists? Average = 1-2%. Good = 4-8%.

Why it matters: Playlist adds = repeat views. Repeat views = sustained algorithm boost.

**5. Audience Demographics + Retention**

Who's watching? When do they drop off? This reveals content weaknesses.

Why it matters: Different audiences need different content. Retention cliffs reveal pacing issues.
