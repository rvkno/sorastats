### The Hook Window (0-0.5 Seconds)

TikTok's algorithm decides whether to keep showing your video within the first **half second**. If viewers don't hook, the algorithm stops testing your content.

The hook is:

* Visual pattern break (something unexpected appears)
* Text that creates curiosity ("wait for it...")
* Audio that changes suddenly
* A face/person appearing suddenly

Without a hook: 12% chance of 70%+ completion

With a strong hook: 42% chance of 70%+ completion

### Three Formats That Hit 75%+ Completion

**1. "Trend + Twist" (77% Avg Completion)**

Follow a trending audio with an unexpected visual payoff. Example: trendy dance audio but an AI character does the dance perfectly. Viewers stay to see the twist.

**2. "Rapid Pattern Breaks" (74% Avg Completion)**

Repeat a pattern 3-5x then break it unexpectedly. Example: "Things that don't make sense" with 3 illogical AI scenarios shown in quick cuts. Viewers watch for the payoff.

**3. "Story Structure" (75% Avg Completion)**

Setup (problem) → Conflict (complication) → Resolution (payoff). Example: "I interviewed an AI" with unexpected response. Narrative keeps viewers watching.
