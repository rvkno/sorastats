### Three Formats Proven to Drive Saves

**1. Before/After (13.2% Save Rate)**

Side-by-side comparisons stick in memory. Examples: productivity workspace upgrade, AI tool workflow before/after, design transformation. The formula is simple: professional visual quality + one clear insight.

Why AI wins: Generating polished "after" visuals requires hours of production. AI does it in minutes.

**2. Quick Tips (11.8% Save Rate)**

Three to five actionable tips in 20 seconds. "5 AI tools that saved me 10 hours." Users save these for reference.

The secret: text-first design. 60% text, 40% visuals. Reading time = watch time = algorithm fuel.

**3. Aesthetic Storytelling (10.9% Save Rate)**

Consistent color palettes + narrative arc. "A day in the life" with matching aesthetics throughout. Cohesion triggers saves.
